HOW TO PLAY

Goal

Lowest total score after 11 rounds wins. Each round you try to arrange your whole hand into books (3+ cards of the same rank) and runs (3+ consecutive cards of the same suit).

Rounds & wilds

Round 1 deals 3 cards and 3s are wild … up to round 11 with 13 cards and Kings wild. Jokers are always wild. Wild cards can stand in for any card in a book or run.

Your turn

Draw one card from the deck or take the top of the discard pile, then discard one card (not the one you just took from the discard pile).

Going out

If after your discard every card in your hand fits into books/runs, you go out — everyone else gets one last turn, then leftover cards count against them.

Scoring leftovers

3–10 = face value · J=11 · Q=12 · K=13 · round wild = 20 · Joker = 50.

Tips

Watch the green glow — those cards already fit in a meld. The counter shows your best possible leftover points.
